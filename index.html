Why do we do this ?

Water is an essential source for us.
It is our duty and obligation as citizens
of this world to do whatever we can to protect such a valuable resource

Our Mission

We strive to promote water conservation by bringing
attention to unique ways to
conserve, protect and replenish our water supply.
Water is essential for our very existence,
and it is our hope to be a
guide for the youth of this generation in protecting it.
